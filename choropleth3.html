Almonds of Wrath?

Navigation: This is an interactive data vizualization built in D3 and tested using Mozilla Firefox 40.0.3. Use the slider bar to select a year. The map can be further explored by double clicking a region to zoom. Hold the mouse and drag in order to pan.

Data: Polygons represent agriculturally permited boundaries in 2014, based on data from the Kern County Office of Agricultural and Measurement Standards. Cover-type classification of each parcel is assigend using a raster crop cover-type data layer released by the National Agricultural Satistics Service, based primarily on 30-meter resolution Landsat sattelite imagery.
